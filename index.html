Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Cutline ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Email address — collected when you verify your Premium subscription. Used only to validate your subscription status with LemonSqueezy.
  • Web page content — screenshots are captured locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any captured content.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect browsing history, personal information beyond email, financial data, location, or any behavioral data.

Third Party Services

  • LemonSqueezy — handles payment processing. See their privacy policy at lemonsqueezy.com.
  • Google Gemini — used for AI analysis of screenshots. Images are sent to Google's API only when you explicitly request an analysis. See Google's privacy policy at google.com.

Data Storage

All preferences and subscription status are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Changes to This Policy
